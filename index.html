About Me

Greetings! I'm Oksana, though most of my students affectionately know me as Ms. Ana. My journey in early childhood education has been fueled by an unyielding commitment to nurturing the growth and development of young minds. From the outset of my career, I've poured my heart into crafting enriching and empowering experiences for children during their crucial formative years. Through a combination of seasoned expertise and boundless enthusiasm, I strive to create a supportive educational environment where each child can thrive and realize their full potential. Come alongside me as we embark on this transformative journey, shaping the future, one young learner at a time..

Activities

With a heartfelt dedication to supporting children with autism and other special needs, I have long been committed to helping them discover and explore their unique areas of interest. Through personalized private sessions conducted in the comfort of students' homes, I offer a diverse range of activities tailored to enhance their fine-motor, cognitive, and social skills. It is my mission to create an inclusive and enriching educational environment where every child can thrive, irrespective of their challenges, empowering them to reach their fullest potential. Join me as we embark on a journey of discovery and growth, one child at a time.
