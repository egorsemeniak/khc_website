About Me

Greetings! I'm Oksana, though most of my students affectionately know me as Ms. Ana. My passion for education and special education has always been about helping young minds grow and develop. Since the beginning of my career, I've dedicated myself to creating fun and empowering experiences for children during their important early years. With my experience and excitement, I aim to build a nurturing environment where every child can succeed and reach their goals. Join me on this exciting journey as we shape the future.

Activities

I'm excited to announce that I'm offering classes in Art and Craft, Cooking, Lego building, Story Time, and Knitting! These classes can be for small groups or individual sessions and take place at home where students feel comfortable. Each class is 30-60 minutes long, and the price ranges from $50 to $75. Everyone is welcome, regardless of their abilities! Looking forward to having fun and learning together!
